Here are some resources if you are looking for more information on hiking in Anchorage. Hope you enjoy your time in Anchorage and the trails you find.

  • Alaska Parks & Hiking Trails A section of Alaska.org that gives information on these and other trails around Alaska.
  • 10 Favorite Anchorage Hikes They like some of the same hikes, but a few others if you want more ideas.
  • Alaska State Parks Learn about our state parks and find out how to buy an annual pass. A great deal if you plan on hiking in many of our parks.
  • Alaska Life This page has hikes all over Alaska that are shorter than 3 miles.
