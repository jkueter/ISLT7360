The Possibilies are endless: A basic Webpage

When you have to create a webpage, there are so many choices on what you can do.

  • Do you want to make something fun?
  • Or maybe somethings for work?
  • How about someting for your family?
  • Or maybe a hobby or sport you like?

The important part for this class is not the content of the webpage, but how the webpage is created.
Some key things to remember

  1. Function - is it easy for someone to use your page?
  2. Appearance - is it pleasant to look at?
  3. Accessability - can all users access the information?
  4. Navigation - can users find all the information they need from your site?
